## 4. Leaver provisions 🕌

What happens to a shareholder's equity when they leave (issue #101 — "rights of
the one who leaves the company"). Two-category model:

- **Good leaver** (death, disability, mutual agreement, involuntary without
  cause): keeps **vested** shares; company may repurchase **unvested** at
  [nominal / cost].
- **Bad leaver** (voluntary early exit, termination for cause, breach): company
  may repurchase at [cost / lower of cost and fair value].

> 🕌 **Shariah note:** confirm with the sheikh how a departing partner's stake is
> valued and settled, and that repurchase pricing is fair and not punitive in a
> way that conflicts with Islamic partnership principles. Record the ruling here.

## 5. Transfer restrictions

- **Right of first refusal (ROFR):** a shareholder wanting to sell must first
  offer shares to the company / other shareholders.
- **Right to invest / new investors** 🕌 (issue #101 — "how people are allowed to
  invest in us"): new equity issuance requires [board / majority] approval;
  define whether investment must be **equity-based (no interest/riba)** to stay
  Shariah-compliant. **No debt instruments bearing interest.**
- **Drag-along / tag-along:** on a qualifying sale, majority can require minority
  to sell (drag); minority can join a majority sale on equal terms (tag).
