## Why we vest

Vesting protects the team: if a co-founder leaves early, they don't walk away
with a large chunk of equity they haven't earned. It aligns everyone to keep
building. This applies to **all founders**, equally.

## Recommended terms (industry-standard)

| Term | Recommended | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Vesting period | **[4 years]** | Standard for founders |
| Cliff | **[1 year]** | Nothing vests before the 1-year mark; then [25%] vests at once |
| Vesting cadence after cliff | **[Monthly]** (1/48 per month) | Or quarterly |
| Vesting type | **Reverse vesting** | Founders own shares now; company can repurchase **unvested** if they leave |
| Acceleration | See below | |

### Cliff mechanics

- Leave **before** the [1-year] cliff → [0%] vested (company repurchases all).
- Stay **past** the cliff → [25%] vests immediately, remainder vests
  [monthly over the next 3 years].

### Acceleration (optional)

- **Single-trigger** (on acquisition): [X% / none].
- **Double-trigger** (acquisition **and** the founder is let go within [12]
  months): [recommended — accelerate remaining unvested]. Founder-friendly and
  standard.

## Worked example

Founder with **[10,000]** shares, [4-year / 1-year cliff / monthly]:

| Time since start | Vested |
|---|---|
| 6 months | 0 (before cliff) |
| 12 months (cliff) | 2,500 (25%) |
| 24 months | 5,000 (50%) |
| 36 months | 7,500 (75%) |
| 48 months | 10,000 (100%) |
